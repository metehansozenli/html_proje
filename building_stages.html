BUILDING STAGES



  1. Preparation
    2.

    After taking your model out of its box, it should be checked whether there are any missing parts. After reviewing the parts, a road map should be created by examining the booklet thoroughly. Following the steps in the booklet is important to complete model properly. After making these steps if you want you can use water with detergent to clear model’s surface.

        

  2. Building
    3.

    According to montage queue take pieces out of the kit. While taking out use craft knife, then if there is roughness clear it with sandpaper. Before gluing piece check surface whether suitable and then fit it with a few adhesives. Also definitely do not use effective adhesives like super glue. After gluing process finished if there is space fill it with the help of putty then sand it.

            

  3. Painting
    4.

    Painting is one of the most attentions needed and difficult process in making process. In plastic models acrylic or enamel dyes are used and dye’s applying can be make with airbrush or brush. Painting with airbrush gives better results than painting with a brush this is because air brush spreads dye thin and evenly on layers. Applying one ore two coats of primer before the actual painting will allow the dye to adhere better. After undercoating process if you want to dyeing with brush thin out the dye with water and apply in thin layers until you get the colour you want. Wait at least 24 hours between layers. If you are painting with airbrush thin out your dye and apply it on surface thin and evenly.

        

  4. Applying the Decals and Varnishing Process
    5.

    After finished the painting seperate the decals from paper with scissors when you are sure there is not dirt on the model. Keep the decal aproximately 20-25 seconds in the warm water after that put it on the towel and keep waiting. Then wet a place with brush where we will put the decal on the model. When the decal comes where we want you will suck the water on the model with ear sticks and leave to dry it. After dry the decal make one coat of varnish on the decal, this process provide to look better and protect the decals.

            

  5. Exhibiting
    6.

    And happy ending!! You have done your model successfully and now you can exhibit it in a beautiful part of your home.
