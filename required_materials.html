REQUIRED MATERIALS



Model Kit

Hobby Utility Knife

Plastic model kit is the most significant equipment in making cast, model kits can have scales according to the reference model (car, airplane, ship) such as 1/16, 1/24, 1/32, 1/48, 1/72, 1/96, 1/144... On large scale models details and size of the model is larger nonetheless, the creating difficulty of the model also increases.

Hobby utility knife is used in the process of detaching pieces from the kit and trimming excessive parts.



Glue

Dyes

It’s used model pieces for sticking each other. Glues produced for plastic models should be used as glues because using effective adhesive such as super glue can cause deformation on plastic pieces.

In plastic models generally acrylic or enamel dyes are used, before the painting process undercoating is used.



Brush

AirBrush

Is used for in the implementation of the details in the parts and in the application of beginning paint to the model but it may not give good results in complete surface painting.

Is used for on the large surfaces to acquire smooth and flat surface, it gives better results than painting with a brush.



Tweezers

Masking Tape

It is used for hold small parts in the model or to position them better on the surface to be adhered.

It is used to cover parts that shouldn't be painted during the painting.



Decal Solution

Putty

It ensures that the decals are placed on the surface well.

It used to fix the mistakes that can be found in model. After applying it’s smoothed with sandpaper.
